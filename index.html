About Logic Play

Logic Play is a tech-focused club that brings together enthusiasts passionate about Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Data Science. Our mission is to create a vibrant community that builds a strong foundation in computer science while fostering innovation and collaboration.

We Encourage:

  • Collaborative problem-solving
  • Knowledge sharing
  • Skill development through hands-on projects, research collaborations, workshops, and discussions

Outcomes for Members

Develop a strong foundation in core computer science concepts while gaining advanced practical knowledge in AI, ML, and Data Science

Strengthen interpersonal skills like collaboration, leadership, and communication, vital for professional growth

Contribute to research initiatives and real-world technology projects with meaningful impact
